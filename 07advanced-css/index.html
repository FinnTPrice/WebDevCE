What is Pokemon Unite?

Pokemon Unite is a MOBA(Multiplayer Online Battle Arena), where teams of 5 face off to score more points than the other team.

Where can I play Pokemon Unite?
Pokemon Unite is available on Android Devices, along with the Nintendo Swtich 1 and 2.
How do you get points?
You get points by defeating Pokemon, this can be wild Pokemon, enemy Pokemon, or nuetral objectives.
How many Pokemon are in Pokemon Unite?
As of September 28th, 2025, there are 79 playable Pokemon.
