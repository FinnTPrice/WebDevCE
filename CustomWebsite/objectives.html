Neutral Objectives

Almost all objectives grant every team meber who assisted in the KO healing equal to 30% of their max HP (not just the person who registered the last hit) and global exp.
Regidrago does not provide any healing, and Regieleki only grants exp locally.

What does each objective do?

Groudon

What does Groudon Do?

Groudon spawns at the 2:00 mark on Theia Sky Ruins. When a team defeats Groudon, all members of that team will have their atack power and gaol-scoring speed greatly increased.
But not only that, if you KO a Pokemon that's under this effect, the effect will transfer to your Pokemon. That way, even the team that didn't defeat Groudon will have a chance to
keep up, allowing all players to enjoy a heated battle until the time runs out.

Groudon Buff

The user who secures Groudon gains 30 Aeos Energy, while the rest of their team gains 15 Aeos Energy. After securing, Groudon provides a shield and buff to all living teammates,
which quadruples goal-scoring speed and boosts all damage against enemy players. Getting KO'd will transfer the buff to the one who KO'd you. The buff does not expire.

Buff Scaling

Shield Strength: 30% Max HP + 1000 (does not transfer when KO'd)

Damage boost: 50% increase

Scoring boost: 300% increase


Regidrago

What does Regidrago do?

Regidrago spawns at the 8:00 mark on Theia Sky Ruins. When a team defeats Regidrago, the user who secures Regidrago will have an increased goal-scoring speed. After Regidrago
spawns in the center of the map, Regidrago will roam between the top and bottom of the map, staying in the center. Regidrago can either respawn, or an Altaria camp can spawn in its place.

Regidrago buff

Scoring boost: 100% increase

Duration: 60 seconds


Regieleki

What does Regieleki do?

Regieleki spawns at the 7:00 mark on Theia Sky Ruins on either the top or bottom lane. When a team defeats Regieleki, Regieleki will approach the opposing team's nearest goal.
While Regieleki is approaching a goal, it will act hostile towards the opposing team and act as though it was on the team of whomever defeat Regieleki. Regieleki will respawn
3 minutes after it is defeated; it will respawn on the lane it initially spawned in.

Regieleki "Buff"

If Regieleki reaches a goal, it will reward the team who defeat Regieleki points and make the goal defenseless for up to 25 seconds. Depending on how much health Regieleki had upon
reaching the goal, the goal will be defenseless for a set amount of time:

    61-100% Health: 25 Second Vulnerability and 20 Points
    41-60% Health: 20 Second Vulnerability and 16 Points
    21-40% Health: 15 Second Vulnerability and 12 Points
    1-20% Health: 15 Second Vulnerability and 8 Points

Regirock

What does Regirock do?

Regirock spawns at the 7:00 mark on Theia Sky Ruins on either the top or bottom lane. When a team defeats Regirock, that team will be awarded with a sheild, and a temporary increase
to their defensive stats. Regirock can respawn 3 minutes after it is defeated; it will respawn on the lane it initially spawned in; any of Regirock, Regice, and Registeel can spawn in
place of any of them.

Regirock Buff

After securing, Regirock grants a shield based on Pokemon's max HP to the living teammates, along with increasing their Def and SpD

Buff Scaling

Sheild: 8% Max HP for 30 seconds

Defensive Buff: 30% increase to Defense, 25% increase to Special Defense for 90 seconds


Regice

What does Regice do?

Regice spawns at the 7:00 mark on Theia Sky Ruins on either the top or bottom lane. When a team defeats Regice, that team will be awarded with a sheild, and slowly regenerate health overtime
Regice can respawn 3 minutes after it is defeated; it will respawn on the lane it initially spawned in; any of Regirock, Regice, and Registeel can spawn in
place of any of them.

Regice Buff

After securing, Regice grants a shield based on Pokemon's max HP to the living teammates, along with restoring hp overtime to the teammates.

Regice Buff Scaling

Sheild: 8% Max HP for 30 seconds

Healing Buff: Restore 5% max HP every 3 seconds for 90 seconds


Registeel

What does Registeel do?

Registeel spawns at the 7:00 mark on Theia Sky Ruins on either the top or bottom lane. When a team defeats Registeel, that team will be awarded with a sheild, and increaded attack stats.
Registeel can respawn 3 minutes after it is defeated; it will respawn on the lane it initially spawned in; any of Regirock, Regice, and Registeel can spawn in place of any of them.

Registeel Buff

After securing, Registeel grants a shield based on Pokemon's max HP to the living teammates, along with a temporary increase to attack stats.

Registeel Buff Scaling

Sheild: 8% Max HP for 30 seconds

Offensive Buff: 15% increase to Atk and SpA for 90 seconds.
